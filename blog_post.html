The Ultimate Mangalorean Food Tour: Ghee Roast to Kori Rotti

By: **Local Foodie Rakesh** | Published: **November 15, 2025** | Category: **Cuisine**

Mangalore is heaven for those who love coastal spice and rich flavors. Our food is heavily influenced by the Tulu and Konkani traditions, resulting in dishes that are both fiery and comforting. No trip is complete without trying these classics.

Must-Try Dishes

  • **Chicken Ghee Roast:** A rich, red, spicy curry cooked in pure ghee. Unforgettable.
  • **Kori Rotti:** Crispy wafers served with a spicy chicken curry. The iconic Tulu combination.
  • **Neer Dosa:** Feather-light, watery pancakes, often paired with fish curry.
