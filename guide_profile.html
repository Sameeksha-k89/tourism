Expertise & Bio

Anjali is a fourth-generation Mangalorean with a passion for preserving and sharing the traditions of the Tulu Nadu region. She specializes in the architecture of the ancient temples, the stories behind local festivals, and the connection between the sea and the people.

Fluent in Tulu, Konkani, Kannada, and English. Certified local guide since 2018.

Traveler Recommendations

  • **Best time for culture:** Visit during the *Bhootha Kola* season (December-May) for unique experiences.
  • **Must-Try:** A personalized food walk through the old market area.
