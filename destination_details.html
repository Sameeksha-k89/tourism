Sultan Battery: A Historical Watchtower

Built by Tipu Sultan in 1784, the Sultan Battery was constructed to intercept enemy warships entering the Gurupura River. Today, it stands as a rugged piece of history, offering fantastic panoramic views of the estuary.

Visitor Information

The site is calm and less crowded than the beaches, making it a perfect spot for history buffs and photographers. You can also take a short boat ride from the base.

How to Explore

  • Climb to the top for the best river view.
  • Visit at sunset for stunning colors.
  • Check out the small jetty nearby for ferry services.
