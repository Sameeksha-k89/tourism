The Unique Charm of Tulu Nadu

Kudla is significant not just as a port city, but as the heart of Tulu culture. It is the only place you can experience authentic *Kori Rotti, Bootha Kola* rituals, and the rich history of the *Kadri Manjunath Temple*.
